AuroraSMS is a clean-room Android messaging app built for private,
local-first use. It has no ads, analytics, trackers, accounts, or Internet
permission. Android's Telephony provider remains the authority for messages.

The app includes a paged inbox and conversation view, complete-history local
indexing and search, durable drafts and one-part SMS sending, scheduled and
short-delay sending, message deletion safeguards, notification replies and
reminders, local explainable spam warnings, encrypted backup and restore,
appearance profiles, per-conversation wallpapers, and Android Auto messaging
notification metadata.

General incoming and group MMS remain unavailable. This source is still a
pre-release, and carrier, OEM, physical-device, accessibility, and
release-signing gates are not complete. AuroraSMS does not provide RCS. Review
the current status and known limitations before using it as a primary messaging
app.
