AuroraSMS is a clean-room Android messaging app built for private,
local-first use. It has no ads, analytics, trackers, accounts, or Internet
permission. Android's Telephony provider remains the authority for messages.

The app includes a paged inbox and conversation view, complete-history local
indexing and search, durable drafts and existing-thread SMS/MMS sending,
scheduled and short-delay SMS, message deletion safeguards, notification
replies and reminders, local explainable spam warnings, encrypted backup and
restore, appearance profiles, per-conversation wallpapers, and Android Auto
messaging notification metadata.

Bounded synthetic incoming and general/group outgoing MMS paths are implemented,
but physical carrier/OEM acceptance is not complete. New chat/first-contact
compose, archive/pin and other required product surfaces also remain unfinished.
This source is still a pre-release; physical-device, accessibility, performance,
release-signing, and publication gates remain open. AuroraSMS does not provide
RCS. Review the current status and known limitations before using it as a
primary messaging app.
